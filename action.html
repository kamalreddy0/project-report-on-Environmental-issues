How to Help


One of the most important things we can do to protect trees is to plant more of them. This helps to offset the loss of trees due to natural causes, such as disease and old age, as well as human activities like development and deforestation. We can also support organizations that work to protect and conserve forests, and advocate for policies that prioritize the preservation of trees and other vegetation. In our own communities, we can help to maintain and care for the trees by watering them during dry periods, removing trash and litter that can harm them, and avoiding activities that can damage their roots or branches. By taking these actions, we can help to ensure that trees continue to thrive and provide the many benefits they offer to the environment and to us.


Mr.Beast's TeamTrees is a fundraising campaign started by popular YouTube personality Mr.Beast, in partnership with the non-profit organization Arbor Day Foundation, to raise money to plant 20 million trees by the year 2020. The campaign was launched in October 2019 and has since raised over $20 million from individuals, businesses, and organizations around the world.


The Arbor Day Foundation is a nonprofit organization that promotes the planting and care of trees. The organization was founded in 1972 and is headquartered in Nebraska City, Nebraska. The foundation educates people about the importance of trees and how they benefit the environment, wildlife, and communities. The organization also provides resources and tools for individuals and organizations to plant and care for trees. The foundation also partners with other organizations and governments to plant trees and promote sustainable forestry practices. On Arbor Day, the foundation leads a nationwide effort to celebrate trees and encourage people to plant and care for them.


One Tree Planted is a non-profit organization that focuses on global reforestation. The organization works with individuals, businesses, and governments to plant trees in areas that have been impacted by deforestation, natural disasters, or other environmental challenges. The organization partners with reforestation organizations around the world to identify areas in need of tree planting, and works with local communities to ensure that the trees are properly cared for and maintained. Through its efforts, One Tree Planted has helped to plant millions of trees in over thirty countries around the world.
